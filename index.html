Company Started

 How Do We Start

We started this business from farmers in our area who are not able to sell their crops properly and properly. Armed with innovation, knowledge and experience in local and international trade, we have been trusted by producers and farmers to market and sell spices.

Our company grows and develops until requests from various countries begin to arrive. We are now growing with the support and support of clients who have worked with us and we will always keep our products to be the best for our clients. We provide tropical spices such as vanilla, cocoa, cloves and mangosteen fruit.

We are here to provide solutions by accommodating all the results of our fostered farmers so that they can focus on the best agricultural products so that they thrive regardless of how the yields will be. Supported by abundant natural resources by educating fostered farmers, and encouraging the government to export natural products and commodities, we are optimistic that we can provide superior products both in terms of quality and value. With what we have, we are able to meet the satisfaction and expectations to compete in the international market. Tropical Agro Indonesia.
